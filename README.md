# Junteng Liu

**Junteng Liu** is a first-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, working in the **NLP Group** under the supervision of **Professor Junxian He**. He completed his B.Eng. in Computer Science at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024.

## Research Interests

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**

## Publications

Junteng has published **6 papers** as first author or co-author at top venues including NeurIPS, ICML, and EMNLP:

1. **SynLogic: Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond** (2025) — First author. Published on Arxiv.
2. **On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding** (2025) — First author. Published on Arxiv.
3. **On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs** (2024) — First author. Published at EMNLP 2024.
4. **In-Context Sharpness as Alerts: An Inner Representation Perspective for Hallucination Mitigation** (2024) — Co-author. Published at ICML 2024.
5. **C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models** (2023) — Co-author. Published at NeurIPS 2023.
6. **Composing Parameter-Efficient Modules with Arithmetic Operations** (2023) — Co-author. Published at NeurIPS 2023.
