# Personal Information

I am a first-year PhD candidate in the [NLP Group](https://ust.hk/nlp) at [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://www.ust.hk/en). My research focuses on **natural language processing** and **machine learning**, with specific interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
