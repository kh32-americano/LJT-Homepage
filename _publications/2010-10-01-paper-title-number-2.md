Chart understanding is a challenging task for vision-language models due to the need for accurate perception of visual elements and reasoning about data relationships. This work identifies a perception bottleneck where models struggle to extract precise numerical and structural information from charts. We introduce a new benchmark and a method that enhances perception capabilities, leading to improved performance on chart QA tasks.
