Parameter‑efficient fine‑tuning (PEFT) methods adapt large language models with a small number of trainable parameters. This work introduces a compositional framework where PEFT modules can be combined through arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, interpolation) to create new modules for unseen tasks. The approach preserves efficiency while enabling flexible transfer and multi‑task adaptation.
