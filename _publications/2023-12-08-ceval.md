This paper presents C-Eval, a comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models across multiple levels and disciplines, providing a benchmark for evaluating model performance on Chinese language tasks.

Authors: Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He.
