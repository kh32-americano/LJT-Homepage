This paper proposes using in-context sharpness as an alert mechanism for hallucination mitigation from an inner representation perspective, providing a new approach for detecting and mitigating hallucinations in language models.

Authors: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He.
