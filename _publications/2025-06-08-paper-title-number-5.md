C-Eval is a carefully constructed evaluation suite that tests foundation models across a wide range of Chinese language tasks, spanning from elementary to professional levels and covering disciplines such as STEM, humanities, and social sciences. The benchmark provides a nuanced assessment of model capabilities and reveals performance gaps that guide future model development.
