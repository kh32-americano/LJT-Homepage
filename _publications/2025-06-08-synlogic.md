This paper presents a method for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale for learning logical reasoning and beyond. The method generates high-quality reasoning traces that can be used to train and evaluate language models on logical reasoning tasks.

Co-authors: Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He.
